**WalletWise** is the smart, minimalist reminder tool designed for people who want to stay organized without handing over their banking credentials to a third party.

## Why WalletWise?

Most finance apps demand to "connect" to your accounts, scraping your personal data and storing it in the cloud. We believe your financial data should be for your eyes only. WalletWise stores everything locally on your device. No cloud, no data mining, and absolutely no bank login required.

Whether you're looking to eliminate late fees once and for all or you're already a responsible cardholder seeking a better way to stay on top of your monthly payments, WalletWise gives you the clarity you need.

## Key Features

- **Predictive Reminders** — Once you set your initial date, WalletWise intelligently calculates your next deadline so you're always prepared.
- **Privacy-First** — Your data stays on your iPhone. Aethernum LLC never sees, tracks, or sells your data.
- **Reliable Clarity** — Eliminate the frustration of "syncing with your bank" errors. Your data is always available, even offline.
- **Clean Dashboard** — A beautiful, intuitive interface designed for financial clarity at a glance.
- **Smart One-Time Setup** — Enter your card name and next due date once. WalletWise's smart logic automatically calculates and schedules future reminders based on your billing cycle.
