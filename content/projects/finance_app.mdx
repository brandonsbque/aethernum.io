We are creating a lightweight application that will help users with their finances. Users will not be required to input any sensitive data, such as bank login information that many apps ask for in order to work properly.

The objective of this application is to enable users to set everything up just once. After that, they can forget about the app, and let it do its thing - reminding the user for due dates, subscriptions, etc.
